Search queries represent the highest-intent signal shoppers provide, yet most stores fail to act on this data through email marketing. AI-powered search abandonment emails should recall the shopper's specific query with contextually relevant products rather than generic promotional messages. This behavioral trigger belongs alongside cart and browse abandonment as a core recovery mechanism most ecommerce teams have yet to implement.
