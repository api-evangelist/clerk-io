Identifying at-risk VIP customers requires monitoring behavioral changes like stretched purchase intervals, missed reorders, and dropped engagement rather than relying on static segmentation. Effective win-back strategies should focus on relevance by showing customers products aligned with their purchase history rather than discount-heavy campaigns, with AI email agents enabling personalized outreach at scale.
