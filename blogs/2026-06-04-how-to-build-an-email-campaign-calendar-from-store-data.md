Traditional email campaign planning built through team meetings and blank spreadsheets arrives at decisions too late; AI can instead create initial campaign calendars using actual store data like inventory levels, customer segments, and past performance metrics. The marketer still approves every step, but the first draft is no longer a blank spreadsheet, allowing teams to focus on strategic decisions rather than manual data synthesis.
