E-commerce brands can improve email marketing by maintaining consistent campaign themes while personalizing product selections for each recipient. An AI email agent automatically assembles product recommendations based on individual shopping behavior, browse history, and purchase patterns, transforming generic newsletters into a personalized storefront inside the inbox where a runner and yogi receive entirely different product blocks within the same campaign.
